Countries with iLETS Systems

iLETS went online in March, 2001. As yet, there are no countries with iLETS capability.

iLETS is programmed in Perl, and is open-source. We encourage anyone with an interest in Perl to play with the program and make improvements, for which your contribution will be noted.
